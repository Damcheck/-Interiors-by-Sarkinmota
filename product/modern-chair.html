Frequently Asked Questions

What does the interior design process look like?

Our process begins with a consultation to understand your vision, needs, and budget. From there, we create design concepts and provide 3D renderings for a clear visual of the project.

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines can vary depending on the scope. For example, a single-room redesign might take 4–6 weeks, while a full home renovation could take several months.

Can you work within my budget?

Absolutely! We believe in creating beautiful spaces that are achievable within any budget. During the planning phase, we’ll discuss your budget openly and prioritize elements to maximize value and style.

Do you offer virtual design consultations?

Yes, we do! We offer virtual consultations for clients who prefer remote collaboration or are located outside our immediate service area.
